# Business Insights

## Workforce Distribution
Production department contributes the largest share of employees in the organization.

## Attrition Analysis
The Production department also experiences the highest employee attrition.

## Employee Demographics
Most employees belong to the 35–45 age group.

## Performance Analysis
A large percentage of employees fall under the “Fully Meets” performance category.

## Employee Engagement
Departments with lower engagement scores tend to show higher absenteeism.

## Hiring Trend
The organization experienced peak hiring between 2011 and 2014.
